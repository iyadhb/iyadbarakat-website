About

A strategist at the intersection of technology and human judgment.

Iyad Barakat is a digital transformation and consulting leader with more than 26 years of experience helping organizations navigate change — most recently with EY MENA, and previously across nearly three decades in London.

A strategist with executive education in digital transformation and disruption from the University of Cambridge, plus executive training from MIT and Emeritus in design thinking, he has spent his career at the intersection of technology, strategy, and human judgment.

Iyad has appeared on television and radio and written extensively on digital transformation and innovation. Palestinian-American and now based in the Middle East after 28 years in London, he brings a genuinely global lens to how consulting, technology, and the Middle East intersect.
